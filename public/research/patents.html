Patent Portfolio

Ten provisional applications filed January 2026

Forged Lucidity maintains a portfolio of ten provisional patent applications covering the operational tools built on the Cx framework. The research itself is published openly. The engineering — how we turn the theory into working systems — is protected.

Guiding principle: The WHAT and WHY are shared freely. The HOW is protected. This is how a cooperative sustains itself — the framework belongs to everyone, the tools we build to implement it fund the organization and its members.

Portfolio Strategy

All ten applications are provisional, filed January 2026, establishing priority dates. The portfolio is managed directly by the founder, whose legal background in intellectual property provides strategic oversight without external counsel dependency.

A restricted use covenant attaches to all IP: no offensive weapons, no autonomous kill chains, no mass surveillance. This covenant travels with every license and transfer. It cannot be stripped without supermajority amendment of the operating agreement.
