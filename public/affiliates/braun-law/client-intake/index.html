Thank you for the opportunity to represent you. This letter sets forth the terms of my engagement as your attorney. Please review it carefully and contact me with any questions before signing.

Scope of Representation

My representation will include providing legal advice and counsel on the matter described in your intake submission above, and such other related matters as may arise in connection with this engagement. My representation is limited to those matters for which you specifically request my assistance and does not include representation in litigation without a separate engagement agreement.

Fees

My hourly rate for this engagement is $300.00 per hour. I will invoice on a monthly basis for services rendered. Invoices are due and payable within thirty (30) days of receipt.

Collection of Unpaid Fees and Costs

In the event that any invoice remains unpaid for more than thirty (30) days from the date of the invoice, the outstanding balance shall accrue interest at the rate of twelve percent (12%) per annum from the date the invoice was first issued until paid in full. If it becomes necessary to pursue collection of any unpaid fees, costs, or expenses, I reserve the right to pursue all available legal remedies, including but not limited to filing suit in a court of competent jurisdiction. In the event of collection, you agree to pay all reasonable costs of collection, including court costs and reasonable attorney's fees incurred in connection with the collection effort. This provision survives the termination of this engagement.

Communication

All correspondence and communications should be directed to me at gregory@braunlaw.us or (207) 346-4140. My mailing address is PO Box 107, Westbrook, ME 04092.

File Retention — Rule 1.15, Maine Rules of Professional Conduct

At the conclusion of any matter, I will retain your files for a period of eight (8) years, after which they may be destroyed without further notice unless they have intrinsic value.

I look forward to working with you. Please do not hesitate to contact me with any questions about the terms of this engagement.
